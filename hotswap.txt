- Download and use IntelliJ's JCEF JDK distribution
- Download latest hotswap agent jar from https://github.com/HotswapProjects/HotswapAgent/releases
- Copy hotswap agent jar to $HOME/.jdks/jcefjdk.../lib/hotswap/hotswap-agent.jar
- Add -XX:+AllowEnhancedClassRedefinition -XX:HotswapAgent=fatjar to JVM arguments
